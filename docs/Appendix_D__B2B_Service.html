Appendix D – B2B RapidPay Service

B2B RapidPay Service (RPS) is a powerful revenue model where RapidPay licenses or integrates its platform directly into other businesses’ ecosystems, allowing them to leverage the velocity-based payments infrastructure for their own customer bases. This turns RapidPay into a platform, not just a product.

What is RapidPay Service?

  • Create customized, time-sensitive incentive wallets for their users.
  • Access RapidPay’s decay engine, top-up logic, and spend urgency tools.
  • Offer exclusive deal pools through their own front-end, while RapidPay powers the backend.
  • Track customer behavioral analytics based on time-to-spend, velocity, and value decay.

Target Partners

  • Retailers or marketplaces wanting to stimulate fast purchases.
  • Loyalty platforms boosting engagement.
  • Fintech platforms adding urgency-based wallets without building from scratch.

Revenue Streams for RapidPay

  • Platform Licensing Fees: Monthly/annual charges, tiered by usage.
  • Transaction Fees (B2B): Percentage of transaction volume.
  • Custom Integrations: Paid setup and deployment for white-labeled solutions.
  • Analytics & API Usage Tiers: Premium pricing for high-volume data and feature access.

Strategic Benefits

  • Recurring revenue with low marginal cost.
  • Wider reach via embedded solutions.
  • High stickiness: businesses don’t easily switch wallet platforms.
  • Enhanced data insight from broader usage.
